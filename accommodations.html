Accomodations

Casual rooms have flat-screen TVs, minifridges, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Some have sitting areas with sofas, while others have chabudai dining tables and tatami mats. Some also have furnished patios with forest views.

There's a low-key buffet restaurant, as well as a karaoke bar and a coffee shop with a terrace. Additional amenities include a spa area with massage and body treatments, plus a hot spring, an indoor pool and a sauna.
